Who  We  Are

AppSource is a Philippine-based IT service and consulting company that started out only with IT staffing and 3 employees in their Ortigas Office. It quickly grew over years and now has two additional business units, namely Apps Development and Infrastructure Solutions, and over 60 employees. With a recent reliable source funding and an increase of clients, AppSource has become more aggressive and is set to make waves in the IT world.

Management

Myki  Cantero

Chairman and President



Myki graduated from the University of the Philippines with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. His thesis focusing on Computer Aided Instruction, gained the Most Outstanding Thesis Award from his College. The same work earned him a corresponding award from the Department of Science and Technology (DOST.)

Prior to Decimal Solutions, he was the IT Department Head for the University of Asia & the Pacific, as well as the Center for Research and Communication. As one of the first Microsoft Certified Systems Engineers (MCSE) in the country, he is also an accomplished trainer having delivered technical training to the Information Systems Departments of Coca-Cola, ABS-CBN, Mitsubishi Motors, Concrete Aggregates, and the Asian Development Bank to name a few.

Myki has over 18 years of experience in developing business solutions for corporate clients. Fluent in several environments, he has occupied various technical and leadership positions throughout his career.

Ramon  Maronilla

Director for Business Development (Asia)



Ramon is the Executive Director of J.P. Morgan Asset Management Ltd. in Hong Kong. He previously was the Vice President of State Street Global Advisors Asia Limited and was a senior member of the firm’s Global Fixed Income portfolio management team. His main responsibilities include product development and coverage of SSgA’s fixed income investment capabilities in Asia ex-Japan.

From 2000 to 2004, Ramon was with PIMCO, where he was responsible for institutional client servicing and building the firm’s mutual fund business in the Asian region. Before this, he was a Mortgage Backed Securities analyst at Goldstein Capital Corp., a fixed-income hedge fund based in New York City. Prior to joining SSgA, Ramon also had a brief stint with HSBC Asset Management (Hong Kong) Ltd., where he was responsible for institutional business development for Asia.

Ramon has over 14 years of banking and investment experience and holds an MBA from Columbia University and an Economics degree from the University of the Philippines.

Yari  Miralao

Director for Business Development
(North America)


Yari is Director at The AES Corporation in the United States. His main responsibilities are to pursue greenfield development and acquisitions of power generation assets in the region - the most recent of which is the US$930 million acquisition of the 600-MW Masinloc coal-fired thermal power plant in the Philippines.

From 2003 to 2007, Yari worked with the Global Risk Management Group at AES headquarters in Arlington Virginia. Prior to this, Yari worked as a natural gas trader for the New York based Hess Corporation and as a Money Market trader for local investment house Philippine Commercial Capital, Inc. Yari is currently serving on the Board of Directors of AES’s Philippine entities Masinloc Power Partners Co. Ltd. and Alpha Water and Realty Services Corp.

Yari holds an MBA from the University of Maryland and a Business Economics degree from the University of the Philippines.
